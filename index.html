Instructions!!


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    3.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. When you click on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You will lose the game if your total score exceeds the random number.
  6. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
